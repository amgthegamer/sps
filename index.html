ABOUT SCHOOL

Sobtis Public School is a co-educational day school that fosters the holistic development of each and every student right from early childhood to class 12th. In addition to a stimulating and caring environment, the school offers value-based education modeled on CBSE curriculum with high quality standards and benchmarks. Strongly believing in the fact that the future of the country is shaped in the classrooms of today, Sobtis Public School envisages to train its students in values that are often found conspicuously missing in the contemporary society at large. The school uniquely combines both contemporary and conventional methods in a flexible manner according to the age, inclination and aptitude of the child. The school is backed by state-of-the-art classrooms with Smart Boards, Library, Science Labs, Audio Visual Education, School Counseling Centre, Enrichment Resource Centre, Computer Education, Physical Education, Value Education and Cultural Activities that add additional value to the education it imparts. The Schools' sports facilities consist of a 25 meter Swimming Pool, Cricket Practice Pitches, an Astro-turf Basket Ball & Lawn Tennis Court and Table Tennis Hall. In order to promote cultural activities there is also an Indoor Auditorium and Amphitheatre with Green Rooms, Dance, Music and Arts & Crafts facilities are also provided. Regular Disaster Management drills are conducted to promote safety awareness amongst the children. Leadership summits, debates, quiz and elocution competitions are also regularly held for confidence building.
